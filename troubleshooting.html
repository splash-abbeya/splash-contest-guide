Quick tips before you open a ticket
Invite not arriving

Have your player search for noreply@splashsports.com in spam/junk. Or share the join link directly via text — it bypasses email entirely.

Picks not showing

Ask the player to hard-refresh the page (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows). If the deadline hasn't passed, picks can still be submitted.

Score looks wrong

Check the player's entry detail page — it shows exactly what was recorded and how it scored. Allow up to 15 min after games end for live scores to finalize.

Can't find the contest

Make sure the player is logged into the same email that received the invite. Go to My Contests after login — it should appear under "Active."
