New to Splash? You're in the right place.

Your commissioner set up a contest for you. Here's everything you need to join, make picks, and compete.

1
Create a free Splash Sports account

Go to app.splashsports.com and sign up. It's free — you only pay an entry fee if your commissioner set one up. Use the same email address your commissioner invited you with, or sign up fresh and join via the invite link.

2
Click the invite link your commissioner sent

Your commissioner will share an invite link via text, email, or group chat. Click it while logged into your Splash account. If you got an email invite, check your Spam folder for an email from noreply@splashsports.com.

3
Pay your entry fee (if there is one)

If the contest has an entry fee, you'll be prompted to pay when you join. If the entry fee is $0 or your commissioner is collecting money themselves, just click to confirm your entry — no payment needed through Splash.

4
Make your picks before the deadline

Once you're in the contest, head to My Contests and click your contest to make your picks. Picks lock before the first game of each week or round — ask your commissioner for the exact deadline. Submit early!

5
Track the leaderboard

After games are played, scores update in near real-time. Open your contest in My Contests to see where you stand. You'll get email updates when results are posted.

6
Collect your winnings 🏆

If Splash is handling payouts, winnings are sent to your Splash account within 3–5 business days after the contest ends. Make sure your payment info is set up under Account Settings. If your commissioner is handling payouts themselves, they'll reach out directly.

💡 Missed the invite email? Ask your commissioner to resend the invite link, or have them share it directly via text. The link is the fastest way in — no email needed.
Common Questions
When do picks lock? ▾
Picks lock before the first game of each week, round, or tournament depending on the format. Your commissioner should tell you the deadline — when in doubt, submit as soon as you can. You can change your picks any time before the deadline.
Can I change my pick after submitting? ▾
Yes — you can change your picks as many times as you want before the pick deadline. Once the deadline passes and games begin, picks are locked for that week/round.
I forgot to make picks — what happens? ▾
It depends on the contest format. In Survivor, missing a week typically counts as a loss and you're eliminated. In Pick'Em, you score 0 points for that week. Set a reminder on your phone for the day before picks lock.
I can't find the contest in my account — where is it? ▾
Go to My Contests in the top navigation after logging in. If it's not there, make sure you're logged into the same account you used when joining — you may have multiple accounts if you signed up with different emails.
How do I get paid if I win? ▾
For Splash-managed contests: winnings are deposited to your Splash account within 3–5 business days after the final result. Make sure your payment method is set up in Account Settings. For self-managed contests, your commissioner will handle payouts directly — usually via cash, Venmo, or whatever your group agreed to.
I think my score is wrong — what do I do? ▾
First, check your entry detail page — it shows exactly what picks were recorded and how they scored. Scores can have a 5–15 minute delay during live games. If it still looks wrong after the game has been final for 24 hours, let your commissioner know so they can contact Splash support.
